About me

I am a PhD graduate in biomedical engineering. I research surfaces and biointerfaces (interfaces between artificial surfaces and cells or biomolecules).

On the side, I enjoy developing simple web applications and (more recently) explore the application of prediction algorithms to medical technologies. Either through the development of communication apps or constructing intelligent analytical methods, I believe that healthcare technologies can be improved using emerging tech trends such as cloud services and/or machine learning.
