Elisa Mendoza Zamarripa / PhD

Biomedical Researcher and Front-end Developer

I am a biomedical engineering with a PhD from Tokyo Institute of Technology. I research surfaces and interfaces with a focus on interfacial interactions responsible for the bioinertness of anti-biofouling materials.

I also enjoy developing web applications for healthcare services using emerging tech trends such as cloud computing and ReactJS.

I am enthusiastic for a mixture of science and data design. In the near future, I'd like to explore novel metodologies in data science such as the integration of AI into data visualization for the development of smart healthcare products such as biosensors. Learn more.
