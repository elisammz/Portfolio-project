My PhD. consists of investigating the interfacial water in material surfaces. I use an atomic force microscope to measure the water-induced forces, and vibrational spectroscopy to understand the hydrogen-bonding state of the interfacial water. The scientic goal is to characterize the interfacial water that originates bioinertness. The aim is to design surfaces with bio-antifouling property ("bioinertness") for biomedical and biosensing applications.
